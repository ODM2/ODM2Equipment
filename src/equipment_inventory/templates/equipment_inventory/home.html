ODM2 Equipment Manager

This equipment management web application provides access to and recording of information on equipment inventory and related actions. It is based on the Equipment Extension of Version 2 of the Observations Data Model (ODM2). This interface is designed for monitoring networks using in site sensors to manage physical infrastructure and link it to observational data.
